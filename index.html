What is scrtchpd?

scrtchpd is a simple tool for quick note taking. Just start typing.

  • scrtchpd saves your notes in your browser, locally, so you don't need to worry about it.
  • scrtchpd works best when paired with the New Tab Redirect Chrome extension. Just set up the extension to direct new tabs to scrtchpd.com and you'll have your notes available every time you open a new tab.
  • scrtchpd now supports live Markdown previews, to help organize your thoughts a bit easier. Check out the Markdown Documentation for details. A few quick Examples:

# Headline

## Secondary Headline

**Bold** or _Italic_

* List item

1. Numbered list item

A Cottleston Pie adventure.
